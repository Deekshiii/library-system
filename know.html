DETAILS ABOUT LIBRARY:

1. Libraries are collections of resources that are organized for people to use.
2. Libraries can contain books, magazines, newspapers, journals, audio and video recordings, and other materials.
3. Libraries can be public or private. Public libraries are funded by the government and are open to everyone. Private libraries are owned by individuals or organizations and may be open only to members.
4. Libraries use a classification system to organize their resources. The most common classification system used in libraries is the Dewey Decimal System.
5. Libraries have staff members who can help you find resources, answer questions, and provide other services.
6. Many libraries now offer digital resources, such as e-books, audiobooks, and online databases.
7. To borrow materials from a library, you typically need a library card. You can usually get a library card by showing proof of your identity and address.
